Gentrification Prediction and Analysis

Time Period

Characteristics of gentrified tracts:

  • No major changes in population.
  • Household income rises steadily.
  • Home value rises significantly.
  • Tends to border tracts that have already gentrified.
Red - Gentrified by the end of the speicifed time period;
Green - Eligible to gentrification at the beginning of the time period.
The shading shows the median home value, which rise with gentrification.
